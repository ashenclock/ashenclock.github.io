University of Palermo project focused on pathological speech analysis and low-resource clinical AI.

- **Stack:** Python, PyTorch, Whisper, Parakeet TDT, BERT, XGBoost
- Re-implemented and modernized the framework by Pappagari et al. (2021) replacing legacy ASR components.
- Achieved **87.32%** accuracy, surpassing the original reported result (**84.51%**).
- Compared deep speech embeddings against handcrafted features, validating representation learning for this task.

Repository: [pappagari2021-interspeech-replication](https://github.com/ashenclock/pappagari2021-interspeech-replication)
