Research work on DAIC-WOZ using self-supervised speech representations and temporal modeling.

- **Stack:** Wav2Vec 2.0, Transformers, CNN-1D, Gated Attention Pooling
- Designed a hierarchical model to capture long-term temporal patterns in speech signals.
- Added augmentation with eDAIC samples to reduce class imbalance.
- Outperformed SVM and raw-audio CNN baselines.
