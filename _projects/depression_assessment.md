**Technologies:** Wav2Vec 2.0, Transformers, CNN-1D, Gated Attention Pooling, eDAIC.

Designed a hierarchical SSL architecture combining Wav2Vec 2.0 with Transformer encoders and Gated Attention Pooling to capture long-term temporal dependencies in the DAIC-WOZ dataset.
Implemented Data Augmentation strategies using eDAIC samples to mitigate class imbalance in clinical data. Outperformed SVM baselines and raw-audio CNNs, validating the efficacy of pre-trained Foundation Models.
