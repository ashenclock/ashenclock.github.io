**Technologies:** Unity 3D, C#, LLM Integration, SPARQL.

Developed an autonomous AI Agent acting as a guide in a virtual museum, handling multi-step queries. Implemented a neuro-symbolic approach where an LLM generates SPARQL queries to retrieve facts from an ontology, ensuring accuracy and minimizing hallucinations.
